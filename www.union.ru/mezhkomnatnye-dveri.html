Interior doors

swollen

The catalog features all kinds of premium interior swing, hidden doors and swivel doors, each of which can be put in one of several boxes to choose from, including the company's latest development - the shadow gap box.

Why Architects Choose UNION Doors

  • Reliability and silence: UNION doors are 50% thicker than standard ones, which improves sound insulation and guarantees perfect geometry even at a height of 3.5 meters.
  • The power is in the details: The reinforced boxes are made of hardened aluminum according to Italian technology and are patented by UNION. They are resistant to moisture and will serve you for many years.
  • Perfect comfort.European fittings (hidden loops INVISTA/OTLAV, magnetic AGB) provides the most smooth and comfortable use. 
  • Design without borders: The widest selection of models and finishes from environmentally friendly materials will allow you to fit the door into any interior style.
    Our consultant designer will help you understand and choose a model, box, type, color, texture of the canvas finish for your home. Do not miss the opportunity to order doors from the manufacturer according to your size or buy ready-made UNION models at a bargain price from a warehouse in Moscow.
