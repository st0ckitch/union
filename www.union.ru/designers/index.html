Why is cooperation with UNION interesting and profitable?

Doors and furniture in the same style

How much do you need to visit interior salons to pick up doors and furniture for the project?

How to make sure that they are combined not only in style, but also in materials and color? After all, often different manufacturers named the same decor or shade look completely different!

We know this is a significant problem! That is why we offer comprehensive interior solutions in a single style, where doors, furniture, partitions are perfectly combined with each other. The unity of all elements gives completeness and integrity to the interior.

35 years on the market

UNION has been in existence since 1990.

UNION has been in existence since 1990. 35 years of history, experience and professionalism. Recognized leader of the Russian door market. Conductor of new trends, designer of design and technologies, patent holder, manufacturer and reliable partner.

Production in Russia

In 2008, some of the production...

In 2008, part of the production processes were transferred from Italy to Russia. Today, the company has 3 production sites, the total capacity of which is more than 50,000 door paintings and 20,000 pieces of furniture per year.

Price is quality

Own production, technological ...

Own production, technological developments and patents, well-built logistics and cooperation with well-known Italian suppliers (ANSELMI, ECLISSE, AGB, COLOMBO, etc.) allow us to offer Russian customers high-quality products at an optimal price compared to Italian manufacturers.

Italian factories

The company is officially cooperating...

The company officially cooperates with more than 20 leading Italian factories - door manufacturers - GHIZZI & BENATTI, LEGNOFORM, BLUINTERNI, ADIELLE, LAURAMERONI, LONGHI, LUALDI, etc. Representatives of these factories regularly conduct training for UNION salon managers, so the company’s employees are aware of all new products.

Always trendy!

UNION is a long-term guide to the Russian...

UNION is a long-term guide to the Russian market of new destinations. Hidden doors for painting, doors made of aluminum and glass, “stone” doors and many other trendy products were first brought and started to produce in Russia by UNION. Today it is the only domestic company offering its customers doors, partitions and furniture in a single style.

Italian designers

When developing new products, UNION...

In the development of new products, UNION cooperates with famous Italian architects and designers. They develop all models and designs according to the company’s orders. Among the partners of the brand are Francesco Ruffini, Cristiano Bianco and others. Therefore, all UNION products are technological and relevant, always in trend!
