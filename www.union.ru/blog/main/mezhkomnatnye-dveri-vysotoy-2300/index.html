High interior doors

2300 and above. Everything you need to know so as not to make a mistake with the choice

Author: Eugene Bobrov,
doorman
Posted 03.12.2021
Updated 30.01.2026

It so happens that the layout with high doorways “got” you from the developer, but it happens the other way around – your design project provides doors height of 2300 mm or higher, maybe even with the installation “under the ceiling”. Doors with a height of more than 2300 mm (sometimes 2300 too) are considered non-standard and made to order. But what are the pitfalls, how to order the right design and not regret it? We'll tell you what to pay attention to.

Design. How high doors affect the visual perception of the interior: 

  • They raise the ceiling visually.

  • The absence of the upper lintel of the door “to the ceiling” creates a feeling of a single space. ? Watch the video and see it in 10 seconds.)

The picture shows a view from below of the ceiling. A slightly open high interior door "under the ceiling" without an upper jumper.

Interior doors with a height of more than 230 cm differ from doors of "standard" sizes by a number of technical features and advantages: 

  • Special design and material of the box;

  • Thickness of the doorway (A number of manufacturers are available to order cloth increased thickness. Thick canvas, due to special filling, allows, for example, to achieve better sound insulation;

  • Depending on the height and weight of the canvas, the doors are equipped with special hinges in the amount of two or more pieces. Thanks to this, an easy ride and a smooth opening of the door are achieved.

Today, high-tech and minimalism are very popular. For doors of modern styles often use hidden boxes, while not using casings. Especially interesting look doors mounted in the same with the wall or wall panel plane (photo below), such doors are called "complanar". 


A door that forms a single surface (plane) with a wall is called a coplanar door. Such a door has no casings and other protruding elements except the handle and lock.

The right box for the high door. Why does it matter? 

Above the canvas = more weight. The large weight of the canvas creates an increased load on the door box, especially with a sharp slamming of the door. As a result, there may be cracks on the walls, including at the joints. Another possible cause of cracks is the use of wooden boxes or boxes with the presence of wood. The tree can absorb moisture, swell and break the plaster.

Therefore, the box for a high door should be durable, reliable, designed for an increased mass of the door, have a rigid design, reinforcing mesh and a high-quality shock absorbing seal. 


A fully aluminum door box with a false box. Anodized or painted in one of the colors to choose from. Reinforcing mesh, preventing cracks on the finished wall experiencing dynamic loads. Sealer.

The INVISIBLE hidden box (obsolete Chameleon-N) from UNION is a frame made of high-quality aluminum reinforced with a false box (additional side profile/support). Due to this, the design acquires high geometric rigidity.

The reinforcement mesh strengthens the plaster and prevents cracks on the wall experiencing dynamic loads.

It is also very important to make the correct installation. The box must be fixed rigidly. How to make the correct installation of the door You can watch the video in just 3 minutes. 

Thickness and construction of the canvas. 

The average thickness of door paintings from domestic manufacturers is 30-45 mm, MDF panels are most often used in the design of such doors (ordinary panels of medium rigidity, which are most often used by manufacturers of inexpensive doors).

High interior doors UNION have a thickness of 60 mm - for the Russian market phenomenon is new and unusual.

The frame consists of a multi-layer array lined with panels of compacted HDF (High Density Fiberboard), and the design of some doors contains a metal compensation rod. This makes the canvas incredibly strong and reliable even at a height of 350 cm.

Why is it important and what does it do? 

  • The best sound insulation of the door due to increased thickness and filling of the canvas;

  • High resistance of the fabric to possible curvatures, due to good structural rigidity;

  • Improved aesthetics and solid appearance;

  • Cloths with HDF panels are also good because they allow the use of even heavy finishing materials, such as porcelain porcelain, stone veneer, glass and mirrors. Unlimited potential for creativity, if you buy doors for painting (finishing).

European hardware. 

Loops. 

 In the high doors of UNION, Italian-made loops are used - powerful hidden installation mechanisms that are adjustable in three planes and designed for high-weight canvases.

 Depending on the weight and height of the canvas, the type and number of loops are selected. 


Depending on the height of the canvas, the door is equipped with several hidden hinges. The ability to adjust in 3 planes allows you to install perfectly. 

Locks. 

It is better to use high-quality magnetic locks that provide silent closure. The recognized leaders in the production of such castles are Italy and Germany.

Sealer. 

The seal is an inconspicuous detail, but very important. Thanks to the seal, the door closes tighter and quieter. One of the best in the world are German seals with high wear resistance.

 Why is this important and gives the seal? 

  • Improves the sound insulation of the door;

  • Provides a soft closing of the door;

  • Provides a tight fit of the canvas to the box.


SUMMARY 

When buying a high door, you need to be sure of its quality. In UNION, high interior doors can reach a height of 350 cm and can be installed “under the ceiling” without an upper lintel. The height and weight of the cloth should not affect the strength and reliability of the door. High-quality fittings and professional installation of an aluminum box is a guarantee of a long service life of the finished door.
