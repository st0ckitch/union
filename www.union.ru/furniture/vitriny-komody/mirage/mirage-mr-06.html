General information

  • The structure is hardened aluminum.
  • Loops - SALICE (made in Italy) hidden, with a Soft closing system.
  • Guide boxes - BLUM (made in Austria), with a closer, full opening.
  • Glass shelves in a thin aluminum frame.
  • Safe tempered glass, 4 mm thick.
  • Boxes - 100% array of valuable wood. The bottom of the drawers is skinned.
  • Unique mounting of handles, without visible screws.
  • Complete with LED lighting.
  • No visible assembly screws.
  • A large selection of finishes.
  • European components.
