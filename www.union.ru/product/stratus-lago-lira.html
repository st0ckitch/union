STRATUS-LAGO

DECOMA DESIGN

Sliding interior partition with double glass STRATUS-LAGO

Double glass sliding partitions are the perfect solution for creating privacy and comfort in your home or office!
Such partitions can be used in the home interior to divide the space and create cozy areas for rest and work. You will be able to enjoy peace and quiet, as such partitions provide improved noise protection, thanks to double glass, which absorbs sounds and helps create a quiet and calm atmosphere.
