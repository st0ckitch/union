Preparation of gaps

  • Determine the size of the opening (width, height) L/H, taking into account the installation of hinges, as recommended.
    • Attention! Preparing openings for standard door sizes, you save on the cost of doors!
  • Escarpment The opening and walls should be vertical (without deviations) in the finish. The top of the opening should be horizontal (without deviations) in the clean finish.
  • Floors. The door is set to the level of the clean floor. It is necessary:
        - presence of a clean floor;
        - The floors should be flat, without changes.
