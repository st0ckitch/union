STRATUS-SIGMA

DECOMA DESIGN

Sliding interior partition of aluminum and glass on the STRATUS mechanism

The STRATUS-SIGMA sliding partition, created by the Italian architectural studio DECOMA DESIGN, is the perfect combination of functionality, innovative design and high quality.
SIGMA canvases are made of tempered aluminum and glass to choose from: transparent, matte, corrugated or mirror.
The sliding mechanism TERNO SCORREVOLI (made in Italy) ensures the smoothest movement of the canvases. Thanks to the soft closing device SOFT CLOSING (made in Italy), the partition works silently and safely.
STRATUS-SIGMA, model 02, glass safe transparent GT01 Trasparente / transparent corrugated GT05 Sole. Aluminum frame of the canvas in black color AL08 Black. Sliding tricuspid partition in the opening, hidden track in the ceiling.
