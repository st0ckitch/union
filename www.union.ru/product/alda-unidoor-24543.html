You've seen it before.

Looking for the perfect doors for your apartment or house, but faced with the question of which size to choose?
According to experts, it is best to purchase doors of standard sizes.
Standard sizes are considered as follows: width 60, 70, 80 and 90 cm, and height 200, 210 and 240 cm.
Why should we pay attention to them?

  1. More economical. Standard doors are usually cheaper. It is easier for manufacturers to manufacture components of the same size, thereby reducing the cost.
  2. Easy planning. Preparation of openings for doors of standard sizes is easier and cheaper. This is important for builders and they can prepare everything in advance.
  3. Less mistakes. Standard dimensions reduce the risk of errors both in the manufacture of doors and in the preparation of openings.
  4. Perfect proportions. The proportions of the doors are designed by the designers taking into account the standard dimensions of 80 x 210 and 80 x 240 cm. This ensures the most harmonious appearance with the right proportions.
  5. Quick delivery. The manufacturer often keeps in stock popular doors of standard sizes, so the delivery time of such doors is minimal.
  6. Box delivery time. As you know, hidden boxes should be put on the object earlier, at the stage of finishing works. It does not depend on the chosen model of the door. Therefore, it is better to order standard sizes for which the box is always ready for shipment.
  7. Simple replacement. In case of damage to standard size doors, they can be easily replaced. The manufacturer always has blanks of standard products, and you can quickly make a new door.
  8. Reliability and durability. Designs of standard sizes are more reliable in operation, because large sizes of doors increase the risk of changing the geometry of the product due to changes in humidity in the room.
Conclusion:
By choosing standard door sizes, you save money, time and nerves. This is convenient for builders, ensures the reliability of the product and creates a harmonious appearance. Do not forget this when choosing a new door for your home or apartment.
Our advice is to find out in advance at UNION, which doorways you need to prepare, so that standard-sized doors will then fit there.
