  • Preparation of gaps
1. Prepare the necessary dimensions of the opening L/H, taking into account the installation of hinges, according to the recommendations.
Attention! Preparing openings for standard door sizes, you save on the cost of doors!
2. The slopes of the opening and the wall should be vertically (without deviations) in the clean finish. The top of the opening should be horizontal (without deviations) in the clean finish.
3. Floors. The door is set to the level of the clean floor. It is necessary:
